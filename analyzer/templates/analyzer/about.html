About CV Checker

Compare your CV to any job ad—instantly. Get a clean match score and category-based insights so you can tailor your CV with confidence.

Try CV → JD Analysis

What CV Checker does

CV Checker highlights what you already cover, what’s missing, and where to focus edits. We normalise aliases (e.g., T-SQL → SQL) and group skills by category (Programming, Frameworks, APIs, Databases, Cloud, CI/CD, etc.) to give you an objective, fast first pass.

How it works

  1. Upload your CV and the Job Description (or paste text).
  2. Extract & clean terms; unify aliases and remove noise.
  3. Compare by category across core areas (e.g., APIs, Databases, CI/CD).
  4. Score & insights: overall match %, missing skills, and extras.

Walkthrough

What the score means?

70–100%
Strong alignment. Tailor wording & add proofs (projects, metrics).
40–69%
Mixed fit. Fix the top three missing categories first.
0–39%
Low fit. Consider a different role or rewrite using JD language.

Score = unique JD skills you cover ÷ unique JD skills (after alias normalisation). If you add a JD skill that your CV already has, the ratio can remain unchanged—counts rise, percentage may not.
